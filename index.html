BookTok is TikTok's massive book community - a space where millions of readers discover, discuss, and fall in love with books every single day. It's the most influential book marketing platform in recent years.

Videos tagged #BookTok have garnered over 200 billion views, making it a powerhouse for driving book sales and creating bestsellers overnight.

We understand what makes BookTok tick. We know the trends, the tropes readers love, and how to position your book for maximum visibility.
