What is BookTok?

BookTok is TikTok's massive book community - a space where millions of readers discover, discuss, and fall in love with books every single day. It's the most influential book marketing platform in recent years.

Videos tagged #BookTok have garnered over 200 billion views, making it a powerhouse for driving book sales and creating bestsellers overnight. Authors who tap into this community see remarkable results.

We understand what makes BookTok tick. We know the trends, the tropes readers love, and how to position your book for maximum visibility and authentic engagement.

About Green Marbles

Passionate about connecting authors with readers and making publishing dreams come true

Our Story

Green Marbles PR & Publishing was born from a simple belief: every story deserves to find its readers. We saw talented authors struggling to navigate the complex worlds of BookTok marketing and professional publishing, and we knew we could help.

Our name comes from the idea that books are like marbles - each one unique, precious, and capable of rolling into the hands of readers who will treasure them. Our job is to help your story find its path.

Today, we're proud to have helped hundreds of authors achieve their dreams, from first-time writers seeing their words in print to established authors reaching new audiences through BookTok.
